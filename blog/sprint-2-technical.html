What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

The margin is the amount of space between this box and anything else outside of it, such as other boxes or the edge of the page.

The border is the thickness and style of this box's outer rim.

The padding is the amount of space between the border and the content in this box.

How are they used?

In a CSS file or segment, the margin, border, and padding attributes can be used as follows:

margin: TOP RIGHT BOTTOM LEFT;

border: TOP RIGHT BOTTOM LEFT;

padding: TOP RIGHT BOTTOM LEFT;

Further information can be referred to at W3Schools.
